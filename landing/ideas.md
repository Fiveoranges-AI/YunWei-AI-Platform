## Idea A — "Architectural Precision" (Swiss Grid Modernism)

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets contemporary B2B SaaS

**Core Principles:**
1. Strict geometric grid with intentional asymmetric breaks
2. Typography as primary visual element — scale contrast drives hierarchy
3. White and off-white (#F8F7F4) as canvas; steel blue (#2D6EA8) as sole accent
4. Content density varies by section — sparse hero, denser solution cards

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) and warm off-white (#F8F7F4) alternating sections
- Primary accent: Steel blue (#2D6EA8) — from the logo, authoritative and calm
- Navy: (#0F2340) for headings — strong contrast without harsh black
- Muted slate (#64748B) for body text
- Thin rule lines (#E2E8F0) for structural separation

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Left-anchored large display type, right side floating abstract geometric mark
- Solutions: 3-column card grid with subtle left-border accent lines
- Process: Horizontal numbered timeline with large step numbers as background texture
- Asymmetric section widths — some sections bleed to edge, others constrained

**Signature Elements:**
1. Large Roman numeral "V" watermark as section background texture
2. Thin horizontal rule lines with dot accents at intersections
3. Blue diagonal slash motif echoing the logo geometry

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Cards lift with subtle shadow on hover (translateY -4px)
- Section entrance: staggered fade-up animation
- Nav: transparent → frosted glass on scroll

**Animation:**
- Entrance: opacity 0 → 1, translateY 20px → 0, staggered 100ms per element
- Hover: box-shadow deepens, slight scale 1.02
- Number counters animate on scroll into view

**Typography System:**
- Display: Sora 700 — geometric, modern, authoritative
- Body: Manrope 400/500 — readable, professional
- Accent labels: Sora 600 uppercase with 0.12em letter-spacing
- Scale: 72px hero / 48px section / 32px card / 16px body

## Idea C — "Precision Engineering" (Technical Elegance)

**Design Movement:** Technical documentation aesthetic elevated to luxury — think McKinsey meets Linear.app

**Core Principles:**
1. Monochromatic base with single precise accent color
2. Micro-typography details: tight tracking on caps, loose leading on body
3. Structural elements (lines, borders) as design features not decoration
4. Information hierarchy through weight and size, not color

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF) with light blue-gray (#F0F4F8) for alternate sections
- Primary: Brand steel blue (#2B6CB0) — used sparingly for maximum impact
- Headings: Near-black (#0F172A)
- Body: Medium gray (#374151)
- Borders: Very light (#E5EAF0)

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: Centered but with offset decorative elements breaking symmetry
- Full-bleed section dividers using thin 1px lines
- Solutions: Horizontal scrollable cards on mobile, 3-col on desktop
- Process: Large step numbers (200px, very light) as background, content overlaid

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin 1px horizontal rules with small square dots as section separators
2. Monospaced font for technical labels and numbers
3. Diagonal cut section transitions (clip-path) with light blue tint

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Minimal animation — only what serves comprehension
- Hover states: underline reveals, not color changes
- Focus on content legibility above all

**Animation:**
- Subtle: elements fade in with 0.3s ease, no dramatic movement
- Section transitions: gentle opacity change
- Hover: underline grows from left, 0.2s

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk 700 — technical yet refined
- Body: Inter 400 — maximum readability
- Mono accents: JetBrains Mono for numbers/labels
- Scale: 68px hero / 46px section / 30px card / 16px body

## SELECTED DESIGN: Idea A — "Architectural Precision"

**Rationale:** Best matches the brand identity — the logo's geometric diagonal forms, the "V" motif of Five Oranges, and the enterprise consulting positioning. Swiss grid precision communicates trustworthiness and architectural thinking. The steel blue from the logo creates perfect color continuity.

**Key decisions:**
- Light background: White (#FFFFFF) + warm off-white (#F8F7F4) alternating
- No dark backgrounds anywhere
- Fonts: Sora (display) + Manrope (body) from Google Fonts
- Accent: Steel blue #2D6EA8 (matches logo) + Navy #0F2340
- Signature: Roman numeral V watermark, diagonal slash motifs, thin rule lines
